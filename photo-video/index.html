Photography & Videography

Photos and video are taken with a Nikon D750. Animations are created in either Blender or a mix of After Effects and Photoshop. For my animations they start with a storyboard and sketches, then assets and backgrounds are designed and created before adding the movement and actual animation. The Funhouse is a never-ending game that requires two players to stick their heads into a long enclosed wooden box. Players are forced to view this unsettling and never-ending video in different colors and frame rates until they manage to solve the puzzle ... if they can!
