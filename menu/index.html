About

Hello my name is Daphne: artist, pet lover, spooky enthusiast, and unfortunately a Greek deity that was turned into a laurel tree to escape the unwanted pursuits of Apollo.

When I say art is my passion, it truly is- I spent three months planning and executing my graduation cap for my undergraduate degree. I designed and sewed a wand onto my cap with the words "Mischief Managed" and a coded a randomized light sequence with an Arduino and installed the Arduino plus lights into my graduation cap just because the concept excited me. I am in the works of starting a little side shop for resin creations I make in my freetime. Go To About Page
